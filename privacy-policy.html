Privacy Policy

IDG Capital (“we,” “us,” or “our”) recognizes the importance of data protection and is committed to handling personal information responsibly and in accordance with applicable laws, including the GDPR (EU), CCPA/CPRA (United States), and PIPL (China).
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information in connection with our website, events, communications, and other business activities.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal information:
 • Contact and professional information (such as name, email address, company, role)
 • Communications (inquiries and correspondence)
 • Event or business-related information
 • Technical data (IP address, device information, browser type, usage data)
Information may be collected directly from you, automatically through the website, or from lawful third-party sources.

2. How We Use Personal Information

We use personal information to:
 • Operate and improve our website and business activities
 • Communicate with you and respond to inquiries
 • Manage events and professional relationships
 • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
 • Protect security and prevent misuse
Personal information is processed based on consent, contractual necessity, legal obligations, or legitimate interests, as permitted by applicable law.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We may share personal information with:
 • Service providers and professional advisors
 • Affiliates within the IDG Capital group
 • Regulatory or legal authorities where required by law
We do not sell personal information.

4. International Data Transfers

Personal information may be transferred to and processed in jurisdictions outside your country of residence, including China, the United States, and other regions.
Where required by applicable law, we implement appropriate measures to ensure lawful cross-border data transfers and adequate protection of personal information.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy or as required by applicable law.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
 • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
 • Withdraw consent
 • Restrict or object to certain processing
 • Request data portability
Requests may be submitted using the contact details below.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not intended for use by minors, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age required by applicable law.

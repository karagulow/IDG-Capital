about us

A long-term partner for every stage

We support entrepreneurs and companies through the full business lifecycle

Founded in 1993 in the United States, IDG Capital was built on the belief that great ideas can come from anywhere and that visionary entrepreneurs deserve committed partners.

Entering China at a pivotal moment in its transformation, we stood alongside founders determined to create enduring companies and lasting impact.

Today, we bridge East and West with a global perspective, deep local roots, and a community of over 300 like-minded professionals.

As we pursue our goal of becoming a 100-year firm, we remain dedicated to empowering the builders of tomorrow to scale new heights and shape a better future.

“Great companies are built by people before they are built by capital. Our role is to be a long‑term partner, not just an investor.”

Patrick J. McGovern, the Founder of IDG Capital

A journey of many milestones

  • Planting the seeds

    1993-2005

    Founded as the venture capital arm of IDG; entered the Chinese market in the same year, becoming one of the first institutions to conduct private equity investing in the country

    IDG Capital became an independently operated fund, with IDG now a limited partner

  • Nurturing growth

    2006-2009

    Established multiple venture capital and private equity funds with Accel Partners

    Expanded investment scope across stages

  • Deepening roots & Becoming Global

    2010-2015

    Launched the first RMB-denominated fund to better support domestic innovation

    Made our first European investment

  • Coming into our own

    2016-2020

    Expanded into M&A and achieved full independence from IDG

    Strengthened our role as a multi-strategy investment platform

  • Expanding horizons

    2021-Present

    Established secondary market investments to support long-term, flexible growth strategies
